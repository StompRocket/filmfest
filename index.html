About

The Prospect Sierra Short Film Festival is a chance for aspiring film makers to use their skills and show their work. Films will be judged by genre. The top films from each genre will go to the final showing after school along with alumni films! The maximum legnth of submissions must be five minutes including credits. We really hope you have an amazing and fun time! To get more information please contact Brie at bregis@prospectsierra.org.
