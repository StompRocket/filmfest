How to Submit


To submit your film please share your film with Brie. IMPORTANT your film must be at least a 1920 by 1080 resolution and have a file size of no more than 2GB.


Rules and Terms

  • All submissions must be less than 5 minutes.
  • All works must be in English or have English subtitles.
  • Entries can be any genre -- documentary, narrative, fiction, or animation.
  • Entries must be submitted by students and must be completely made by students
  • Adult participation (teachers parents, etc.) should be limited to coaching or acting in the film.
  • All audio/film/graphic content must be original, copyright free or have written permission, and be properly documented.
  • Film must be completed by the deadline date in which they are being submitted. Works in progress will not be accepted, and edits to films after they have been submitted will not be allowed.
  • PSSSFF does not pay screening fees or shipping fees in order to screen a film, nor does PSSSFF underwrite production costs (duplication, subtitling, etc.).
  • Upon completion of the Festival selection process, we will attempt to contact the Applicant using the contact information provided on the submission form to notify the Applicant of the Filmâ€™s acceptance status. It is the sole responsibility of the Applicant to ensure that the contact information provided on the submission form is correct throughout the entirety of the selection period.
  • PSSSFF is under no obligation to provide to the Applicant or any other representative of the Film any comments or feedback regarding the submitted Film should it not be accepted for exhibition at the Festival.

By submitting your films to the PSSSFF, you hereby grant permission for PSSSFF and Brain Storm Studios to use it for the following purposes:


  • Stills: Still images from the films can be used on the website, printed materials, banners, and other promotional materials. Please submit one high-resolution headshot of the filmmaker in JPG format.
  • Clips: Clips of submitted films may be used to promote PSSSFF by appearing on the PSSSFF website, possible partnership websites, third-party advertising sponsors (i.e., television spots, newspaper print, magazine ads, etc.)
